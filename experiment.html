Raphael the Tank Bot

Date Published: 1/10/2024

"Raphael" was a combat robot designed to fight in the 1lb combat robotics competition "Design.Print.Destroy" at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. Tank treads are unconventional in the combat robotics scence, which made this project fun way to explore different drive trains. Upon the left, you are able to see the concept of the Tank bot using CAD for its design.
